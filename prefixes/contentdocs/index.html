Overview

This document defines the set of prefixes that Reading Systems are expected to recognize when used in the EPUBÂ® epub:type attribute in Content Documents.

For more information about prefixes, including how to declare and use them in Content Documents, please refer to Vocabulary Association in the Content Documents specification.

Revision Policy

This document is subject to change at any time. The prefixes defined herein will never be removed, but may be deprecated.

Requests for additions, modifications and clarifications must be made through the EPUB 3 Issue Tracker.
